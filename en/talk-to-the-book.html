Frequently asked questions

What is CoopchAIn?

It is a conceptual proposal that explores how to combine cooperation, blockchain and artificial intelligence to design new forms of social organization in the era of advanced automation.

What does the triple singularity mean?

The convergence of three profound transformations: technological, economic and socio-cultural singularity.

Why is governance the central problem?

Because the question is no longer only what technology exists, but how to design systems capable of balancing decentralization, concentration, coordination and meaning.
